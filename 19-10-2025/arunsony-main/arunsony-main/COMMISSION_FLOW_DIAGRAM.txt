KEY INSIGHTS:
============

1. 🎯 MULTIPLE EARNERS: One payment can generate commissions for multiple people
2. 📈 UPLINE BENEFITS: Higher-level referrers earn from all their downline
3. 💰 TIER-BASED RATES: Commission percentages depend on referrer's tier/level
4. 🔄 SUSTAINABLE INCOME: Active referrers earn from ongoing downline activity
5. 📊 SCALABLE STRUCTURE: More referrals = more commission opportunities
